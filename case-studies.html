How a 6-week automation stack saved 18 hours per week and accelerated invoice collection by 3x

The Client

A Dubai-based trading company focused on import and distribution with a lean operations team managing multiple workflows simultaneously.

The Challenge

The operations manager was drowning in manual, repetitive tasks that consumed 3–4 hours daily:

  • Manually copying WhatsApp leads into spreadsheets
  • Chasing unpaid invoices by phone every single day
  • Typing repetitive follow-up messages to prospects
  • Manually compiling daily sales summaries for the owner

Result: Invoices outstanding for 45+ days, warm leads going cold, and no reliable performance visibility for leadership.

The Solution

Zeno AI implemented a 6-week automation stack with three targeted workflows:

1. Lead Capture & CRM Sync

Automated lead qualification engine using WhatsApp Business API

Automated lead logging into Zoho CRM
Real-time alerts to sales team
Lead scoring and qualification
Multi-channel integration

2. Invoice Chasing Engine

Automated follow-up system for overdue invoices

Monitored overdue invoices in Zoho Books
Automatic WhatsApp & email follow-ups (Day 7, 14, 21)
Escalation process for Day 30
Complete activity logging

3. Daily Owner Briefing

Automated executive summary delivered every morning

Triggered daily at 7:30 AM
Pulls revenue, AR, and orders
AI-generated 5-line summary
Delivered via WhatsApp

Impact Summary

By automating three critical workflows, we transformed the operations manager's role from manual task execution to strategic oversight. The company now operates with systematic efficiency, improved cash flow, and complete performance visibility.

  • Operations manager refocused on strategic initiatives
  • Zero missed leads or follow-ups
  • Predictable invoice collection timelines
  • Daily executive visibility without manual effort
  • Foundation built for expanding automation to other departments
